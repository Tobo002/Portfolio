### Requirements Summary

Your portfolio must include:

1. **About You** - Bio, interests, personal info
2. **How You Work** - Design methodology or current learning journey
3. **Portfolio Sections** - Placeholder pages for case studies with titles and descriptions

### Grading (Devoir 1)

| Component | Weight | Details |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Report Quality | 20% | Clear writing, all requirements addressed |
| Website | 60% | Content, design, responsive, accessible, hosted |
| Feedback Participation | 20% | Active critique in group session |

### Report Submission

Submit a PDF including:
1. Name, student number, title, date
2. JavaScript experience & tutorials followed
3. 3+ portfolio site inspirations (positive & negative)
4. Links to your portfolio & GitHub repo
5. Design rationale:
   - Color palette choices
   - Typography selections
   - Layout & visual hierarchy
   - Personal branding reflection

## 💡 Tips for Success

1. **Start Early** - Don't wait until the last day
2. **Explore Design** - Look at multiple portfolio examples before designing yours
3. **Get Feedback** - Ask peers and TAs for input
4. **Test Responsively** - Check on phone, tablet, and desktop
5. **Document Your Process** - Take notes on design decisions for the report
6. **Follow Standards** - See [RULES.md](../RULES.md) for coding standards
7. **Ask Questions** - Use the forum, don't be shy!
